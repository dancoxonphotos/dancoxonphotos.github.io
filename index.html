About Me

My name is Dan Coxon. I am a Landscape Photographer based in Dublin, Ireland. I have a huge passion for travel and a love for capturing the stunning scenery that the world has to offer.

I found an interest in Photography at a young age, which is when I decided to get my first camera. Ever since then, I have been developing my knowledge in many areas of Photography, such as Portraits and Events, but I have most enjoyed crafting my skill as a Landscape Photographer.

“Photography is like exploring a new dimension, only I can go there but I can show you where I've been.” ― Destin Sparks
